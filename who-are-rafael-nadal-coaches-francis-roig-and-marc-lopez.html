Who are Rafael Nadal coaches Francis Roig and Marc Lopez?

Rafael Nadal is a Spanish professional tennis player, who is currently ranked world No. 4 in singles by the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP).

How many coaches does Rafael Nadal have?

Rafael Nadal officially has three coaches. His uncle Toni Nadal, coached him from 1990 to 2017.

Who are Rafael Nadal coaches Francis Roig and Marc Lopez?

His three coaches are, Francisco Roig, Carlos Moyá, and Marc López.

The longest-serving member of his coaching team is Francisco Roig, who has been coaching Nadal since 2005.

Carlos Moya joined Nadal’s coaching team in 2016, and Marc Lopez joined in 2021.

Roig is a retired professional tennis player from Spain. He is primarily a doubles player, who has won 9 ATP World Tour titles and reached 12 more finals.

Moya is a Spanish former World No. 1 tennis player who, in 2004, was part of his country’s successful Davis Cup team.

He was the French Open singles champion in 1998 and the singles runner-up at the 1997 Australian Open.

Lopez is a Spanish retired professional tennis player and coach whose career-high ATP singles ranking was world No. 106.
