Robbie Amell is a Canadian-American actor, model, and producer. He has played a variety of television roles which include Ronnie Raymond/Firestorm on The CW superhero drama series The Flash, Stephen Jameson in The CW science fiction series The Tomorrow People, Jimmy Madigan in the Nickelodeon teen sitcom True Jackson, VP, Max Miller in the sitcom Life with Derek, Nate “Scooby” Scooberman in the CBS sitcom How I Met Your Mother, Nick in the MTV comedy series Zach Stone Is Gonna Be Famous, Nathan Brown in the sci-fi comedy-drama series Upload, Kevin in the black comedy-drama show A Series of Unfortunate Events, and Special Agent Kyd Miller in the Fox sci-fi drama series The X-Files. Amell has also been cast in films like American Pie Presents: Beta House, The Babysitter: Killer Queen, Resident Evil: Welcome to Raccoon City, When We First Met, and Nine Lives.
