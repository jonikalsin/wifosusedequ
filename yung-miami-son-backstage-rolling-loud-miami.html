Yung Miami Addresses Her Son Being Backstage At Rolling Loud Miami

Yung Miami has addressed the backlash she’s received in the wake of her son being seen at Rolling Loud making it rain on strippers.

The incident started when Miami’s fellow City Girls collaborator JT posted a video of the former’s 10-year-old son backstage at Rolling Loud Miami over the weekend. The video saw Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. throwing some cash on some strippers while surrounded by adults, and many thought the antics were too much for such a young kid.

AD LOADING...

“My son at rolling loud having a ball backstage let’s address it!??” Miami replied on Twitter on Monday (July 24), adding, “That’s why I don’t post my kids y’all bitches can talk a sore in yall mouth about me but I’ll never give y’all the chance to have an opinion about my kids! Idgaf.”

JT responded to the critics too, writing: “Me & Jai do rolling loud together every year! It’s my favorite time cause I get to bond with him & turn up! This was the best year I loved the smile on his face!  I’m a coooool auntie!”

Another guest also paid Yung Miami an unexpected visit backstage, former NFL wide receiver Antonio Brown. The Paradigm rapper posed for a photo with the City Girls rapper at the Miami festival, but didn’t stop there, and used their link-up to shoot his shot at the 29-year-old.

“Told Caresha I’m in the city. She don’t need diddy,” AB wrote.

Despite Brown’s attempt, Yung Miami was later seen partying with Diddy after her show, vibing to the pair’s new track “Act Bad” with JT and Fabolous.

Diddy and Yung Miami’s on and off relationship has served as internet fodder for weeks now. The pair officially broke up back in April but have since been spotted together on multiple occasions, such as at the 2023 Met Gala, where Puff was seen sweating bullets as he was grilled about their relationship on the red carpet.

“We definitely go together real bad. She’s my date for the night, she’s incredible,” he said after initially trying to dodge the question. “We don’t put titles on it. This is like my best friend in the world, one of the most beautiful people God has blessed me to meet, and I’m blessed that she’s my date tonight.”
