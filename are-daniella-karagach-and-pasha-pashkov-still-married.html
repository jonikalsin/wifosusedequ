Are Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov still married?

American dancer Daniella Pashkova was born on December 26, 1992. Daniella was thirty years old at the time that this work was published. Leonid Juashkovsky and Karagach have won the junior I and II Latin championships for the United States three times each. They won the Junior II-10 Dance Championship in 2007. They took part in the Celtic Classic, Moscow Open, and Barcelona Open finals of international tournaments.

In Barcelona, Russia, and Latvia, Karagach and Juashkovsky represented the United States at the world championships. They appeared in seasons two and three of PBS’s America’s Ballroom Challenge. After Leonid, she continued to dance with Pasha Pashkov, and the two of them took home numerous national honors in showdance, Latin, Ballroom, and 10 dance as well as victories in US and international competitions.

They took first place in the UK Open 10-dance, UK Open, and Blackpool Rising Star competitions. When she and Nelly were matched on Dancing with the Stars in 2019, she started dancing professionally. In her third season, she and a well-known partner, Iman Shumpert, went on to win their first Mirrorball trophy.

On the other hand, American ballroom dancer and choreographer Pashkov made his television debut in Season 2 of NBC’s World of Dance. His first dance class was taught by a ballroom instructor at his school when he was eleven years old.

He waited until a mother’s friend contacted and asked if he would be her daughter’s dancing partner before starting to practice again. The major reason I initially stayed was the social scene… You automatically get more driven as you start to experience success.

Pashkov and Daniella Karagach collaborated in 2009. Daniella remembers that “my coaches warned me that people might not respect us as a compatible pair because I was so young because I was a young dancer and he had already competed in the finals.” She had just turned fifteen at the time.

Are Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov still married?

As of the time this article was being written, Daniella and Pasha were still wed. The pair dated for four years before being married on July 18, 2014.
