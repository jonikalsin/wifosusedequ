Meet Shreyas Mehta! His Bio, Age, Job, IG

Meet Shreyas Mehta from Love Without Borders. In 2022, he is a cast member of Love Without Borders where he has been matched with Gurleen Virk, a San Diego native. So, are they still together? What does he do for a living? How old is he? Is he on Instagram or Facebook?

If you’re looking to find the answer to these questions then you’ve come to the right place. Keep scrolling down and find the answers.

Love Without Borders: Are Shreyas Mehta And Gurleen Virk Still Together?

Gurleen Virk and Shreyas Mehta were matched together by Love Without Borders‘s expert Arica Angelo. It is unclear if they are still together after the show because they have never posted each other since then.

In the preview of the show, as Gurleen approaches her match’s residence, she is “panicking inside” but making every effort to maintain “a cool outside appearance.” She claims that she has been wearing the same clothing for more than 20 hours as she makes her way to her match’s place and she hopes she “put on enough deodorant and appear acceptable.”

She continues, “I don’t know who I’m meeting, but I hope and believe it’s the man I want to spend the rest of my life with.” When a girl answers the door, she is taken aback, but things become obvious when she meets Shreyas, who lives with his parents and sister.

Gurleen is not very pleased by the announcement because she believes it will be tough to get to know a man while residing with family. Despite this, Gurleen and Shreyas had a good beginning. It doesn’t last long, though, as Gurleen subsequently feels like she is constantly “failing in love.” Whether they will make it as a couple, only time will tell.

About Gurleen, she is 29 years old and is currently based in San Diego, California. She is working at Google as Responsible Innovation – AI/ML starting in July 2021. Gurleen also did many volunteer works. She volunteered at San Diego Center for Children, UCSD Greek Life, Reading Is Fundamental, San Diego Youth Services, School of Monarch After School Program, Hands On Bay Area, Alternatives in Action, BUILT BY GIRLS, and Oppia Foundation.

Shreyas Mehta Age

In 2022, Shreyas Mehta is 31 years old.

Shreyas Mehta Job

Shreyas is a model and fashion buyer by trade. His ModelMayhem bio reads: “A young boy from India turned to be a man and then a model. Shreyas Mehta stands with a wild and deceptive attitude. Hard work is the religion and principle. Bold, honest, disciplined, and true. I am exactly like a bottle of wine that keeps better and better with time.”

His works include walking for Runway-IIJW 2013, Vikram Phadnis, Umesh Jivnai, and Tassel Designers Awards 2013. He has also appeared in advertisements for Bombay High, Siyarams Vogue, Pantaloons, Print-the-Man, Loreal Paris, Fashion Ethics, Zanosh, and others.

Shreyas’s work has allowed him the opportunity to travel to various places. As his highlights are annotated with recollections from his travels to Paris, Milan, Amsterdam, Barcelona, Madrid, London, and many other nations, Shreyas also appears to be quite an avid traveler.

As a fashion buyer, he has more than 7 years of experience. He worked as a buyer and senior buyer at BMA International FZE since 2014. Moreover, he is a buyer head of Men’s wear since twenty4 fashion since July 2019.

Shreyas attended R N Shah High School in 2007 and studied at m.k.sanghvi from where he graduated in 2012. He completed the University of Mumbai where he studied Mass Media.

Is Shreyas Mehta On IG?

Of course, he is and his Instagram (@shreyas_mehta) serves as his modeling portfolio. He is also on Facebook (@shreyas.mehta.9619).

Shreyas Mehta Family

Shreyas Mehta is the son of Sarita Mehta, a graduate of IES Eng Med School and SVT College of Home Science. According to her LinkedIn, she is working as a free diet consultant and an interior designer. She was born on 7 May 1964.

He has a sister who is also based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. She studied Management at Mithibai College, Bombay (BMS), and graduated in 2016. Furthermore, she was a former Fashion buyer at Splash Fashions.
