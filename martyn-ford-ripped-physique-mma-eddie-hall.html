Martyn Ford showed off his ripped physique ahead of a potential MMA showdown with Eddie Hall in February.

The British bodybuilder looked in pristine shape during a trip to Australia ahead of what promises to be an intriguing step into the world of combat sports in 2024.

Ford posted a picture on Instagram of himself donning just a pair of black swimming shorts whilst on a beach and it appears he’s already in optimal shape for the bout.

Alongside the picture, he wrote: “What a day …. Sun has been blasting, head has been burnt…all in all …success.”

Ford has been linked with a move into combat sports for some time and came close to fighting the Iranian Hulk before it was eventually cancelled.

But he will get his chance next year when taking part in a four-man eliminator which features his friend and fellow strongman, Hall.

Whoever Ford goes to war with will face the ‘World’s Scariest Man’ in full throttle judging by his recent look.

And his social media beach shot is one that can most certainly be believed, unlike that of the Iranian Hulk, real name Sajad Gharibi.

Gharibi had never been seen in Britain and ahead of his debut it became apparent that his size was not as seen in his social media pictures.
