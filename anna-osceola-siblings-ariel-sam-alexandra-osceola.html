Get 2 Know Anna Osceola Siblings: Ariel, Sam, Alexandra Osceola!

Jonathan (Daniel) Hamm, best known for his role as Don Draper in Mad Men, (2007–2015), is now married. For Mad Men, he won the Golden Globe, Primetime Emmy, and many other awards and now he even has a bride because of it. Jonathan just got married (June 2023), to his Mad Men co-star Anna Osceola, at Anderson Canyon in Big Sur, California, where they filmed the show’s 2015 series finale.

Anna, now Mrs. Hamm, was briefly seen as the flower child receptionist at the California coastal retreat that Jonathan’s character stayed at in 2015, in the Mad Men series. Now, in this writing, let us tell you about Anna’s siblings. Anna, 17 years younger than Jonathan, was born on 8 April 1988, in Cambridge, Massachusetts, to her parents. Together, they are a family of six, including four siblings. Now, let us tell you about who these siblings of Anna are.

Who Are Anna Osceola’s Siblings?

Anna Osceola’s four siblings are Ariel Osceola, Alexandra “Alex” Osceola, and Sam Osceola.

This picture from 9 April 2013, posted by Anna Amber, does not include Anna and Alex, as at the time they had not arrived in the world.

Meet Ariel Osceola, Anna Osceola Sister

Anna’s sister Ariel Osceola Barringer was born in May 1978. So, she reached the age of 45 in 2023.

Ariel is married to Channing K Barringer who turned 51 in May 2023. On his IG @channingb, he describes himself as “Sometimes shutterbug. Full-time bringer of joy.”

Anna’s sister studied B.A. in Visual and Environmental Studies at Harvard University in the Class of 2000. She also went to Cambridge Rindge and Latin School.

Speaking of the present, since September 2021 until present, Ariel has been handling affairs as the VP of Operations and Development at Los Angeles-based Launch Pad.

Between October 2015 and May 2018, she was the digital project manager. Even prior, she was in the same position at Disney Interactive and as an account supervisor at Fleishman-Hillard. At one time, she was also Google‘s marketing specialist.

Meet Alexandra Osceola, Anna Osceola Other Sister

Alexandra “Alex” Osceola is another sister of Anna Osceola. She reached the age of 38 in October 2022. One could find her on IG @chayasophia. But, the account was kept private.

On her LinkedIn, she mentioned working as a personal assistant in Los Angeles since 2012. Also, she gave details as to where she studied. Turns out, she went to the Fashion Institute of Technology for a B.S. in Fashion Merchandising Management (2002 – 2006).

Meet Sam Osceola, Anna Osceola Only Brother

Sam Osceola is the only brother of Anna Osceola. He can be found on ‘Sam Osceola’ Facebook, where his last activity was on 23 May 2019. He had made a post about donating to Ian Charles’s birthday fundraiser for St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. His IG @samosceola, meanwhile, was kept private.

Sam was born in November 1981. So, he turned 41 years old in 2022.

Now, here are a few things that we know about his job/career. At the time of this writing, he was working as a managing director and director of finance and development at BRI Capital Partners. Before that, he mentioned working as a donor at World Peace One Foundation (2015 – 2016), as a senior analyst at Vets on Track Foundation (2014 – 2016) and Guggenheim Partners (2003 – 2010).

As for education, Sam got his Business, Finance degree at Emory University – Goizueta Business School. Also, he passed CFA Level 1 and 2 at Cambridge (1999 – 2003).

Related FAQs

  • Where Do Anna Osceola’s Siblings Reside?

Anna Osceola’s sisters Ariel and Alex seemed to be residing in Los Angeles, California, as of June 2023. Sam, on the other hand, mentioned New York, New York as his ‘current residence’ on Facebook. He also marked Cambridge, Massachusetts as his hometown.

  • Are Anna Osceola Siblings Close?

It seems so. Anna Osceola’s parents seem to have brought them up while always reminding them to value and love each other. The Osceolas together look pretty tightly knitted.

Anna’s father is Fuad Osceola. He is a massage therapist in Woodland Hills, California. On his work website, it’s been said that he is an award-winning massage therapist who has been soothing clients with his integrated body-centered therapies for over 30 years. He also is a former psychotherapist, with an M.Ed. in Counseling and Consulting Psychology from Harvard University.

Anna’s mother is Lisa G Osceola. She reached the age of 68 in October 2022. She seemed to be residing in Cambridge, Massachusetts as of now.

Now, finally, here are also a few facts about Anna.

She and Jonathan were first introduced back in 2015, and that same year the latter had called it quits with his 18-year-long partner actress Jennifer Westfeldt. All those years, Jonathan was notably against marriage. To Playboy, back in 2012, he had said his divorcee parents shaped his perception of marriage.

So, Anna and Jonathan’s relationship started growing in 2020. And in September 2022, during an appearance on Howard Stern, the latter hinted at a possible change of heart about getting married. Later, in February, they got engaged.

A native of Cambridge, Massachusetts, Anna is said to have relocated to California to chase her Hollywood dreams. She’s known for her roles in TV series like Rizzoli & Isles, NCIS, and Law & Order.
