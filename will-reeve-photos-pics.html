See Photos Of The Reporter Hollywood Life

Will Reeve is a news reporter, and the son of actor Christopher Reeve and wife Dana Reeve. Will frequently appears as a guest reporter on Good Morning America — including a memorable appearance during the COVID-19 quarantine in April 2020. Will accidentally revealed on camera that he was only wearing boxers at home! Along with GMA, Will has also appeared on shows like ESPN’s SportsCenter. He followed his late father by appearing in several of his films, like In The Gloaming and The Brooke Ellison Story. Will also sits on the board of directors at the Christopher and Dana Reeve Foundation, which benefits those suffering from paralysis, like his father.
