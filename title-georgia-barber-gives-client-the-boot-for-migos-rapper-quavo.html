Georgia Barber Gives Client The Boot For Migos Rapper Quavo

Riverdale, GA - 

Quavo was given the star treatment at a Diamond Cuts Barber & Beauty Studio in Riverdale, Georgia over the weekend — much to the detriment of a fella named Lance. In a clip shared by The Neighborhood Talk, the barber is working on Lance’s hair when the Migos rapper saunters in with his crew.

The barber jokingly says, “Yo, you gotta go Lance. You gotta go.” Lance clearly understands the assignment and starts to laugh before giving his seat up for the platinum-selling artist.

Reactions to the barber’s decision to give Lance the boot have been mixed, but most people weren’t impressed. “Shiddd he would’ve lost a forever client too off that D riding,” one person wrote, while another said, “Isn’t every client suppose to be treated the same? I’m sure boy probably comes more frequently than Q?”

AD LOADING...

Despite the negative feedback, it seemed everyone at Diamond Cuts knew the drill when Quavo walked in and weren’t bothered.

Quavo teamed up with his Migos brethren Takeoff in May for a collaboration called “Hotel Lobby,” the first offering from their Unc & Phew sideo duo. The name is a nod to Quavo and Takeoff’s familial ties (although they are only four years apart). The release arrived just days after Offset apparently unfollowed Quavo and Takeoff on social media. leading to rampant rumors of a split.  The trio then pulled out of the Governors Ball 2022, which only added fuel to the fire.
