Tiffany Young Explains Her Night Time Skin Care Routine

Tiffany Young is revealing how she keeps her complexion looking so incredible.

The 32-year-old Girls Planet 999 judge and Girls’ Generation star shared the tips and tricks for her night time skin care routine with Allure, which consists of around seven steps.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Tiffany Young

Find out what she uses…

To begin to remove her makeup, Tiffany uses the Lancôme Bi-Facil Double-Action Eye Makeup Remover.

She then uses an oil-based cleanser – La Mer’s The Cleansing Oil – to dissolve the rest of her makeup. For a foam cleanser, she uses MAC Lightful C + Coral Grass Vitamin C Cleanser.

Tiffany also exfoliates with a scrub every five to seven days, and says La Mer’s The Replenishing Oil Exfoliator is “one of her longtime favorites.”

Then, it’s onto masks.

“I use around four to six masks monthly to make sure I’m keeping track of time and a budget. Also, I’m very aware of my skin condition, so I [choose] masks for specific needs,” she revealed. Make sure to choose the right one for you – there are plenty of options at Sephora!

Her personal recommendations on a budget are from Dr. Jart and Innisfree. For a more luxurious indulgence, she opts for the Hanacure All-in-One Facial Set.

She then seals it in with an overnight mask, the Laneige Water Sleeping Mask, followed by a lip treatment with Fresh Sugar Lip Serum Advanced Therapy.
